Meet Lily

Lily is a 37-year-old working mother of 14-year old twins, Ben and Zoe, and wife to Michael, who is 42. She and her family have recently relocated from Oregon for Michael's new job with Boeing in their Everett, WA office.

Lily has never had any major health issues and is currently in good health. As the family's healthcare decision maker, she determines where their health-related lifetime value of $3.4M will be spent. She is also the health services confidant of both her father and sister as well as a small circle of close friends. She relies on her laptop and smartphone to stay in touch via text, email and social media, and has just started a blog: Seattle Newbie.

After losing her mother five years ago, her small family has become even closer. Her father Simon, 68, lives in Southern California. Her sister Jenny, 24, lives in Anchorage. Also in her circle of influence is her best friend from college, Johana, who now lives in Missoula, Montana.

New Mover Greeting

Lily and her family just moved to Seattle needing a healthcare provider. She receives an email from Providence highlighting locations near her home. That same day she receives a descriptive mailer from Simply Health including a discount for her first visit.

Selecting a Package with Ease

Michael and Lily review and select new health benefits, and are pleased with the clarity of the material, the comprehensiveness of coverage, and the ease of the process.

Registering With Simplicity

Lily’s family receives a confirmation email about their healthcare coverage. She is pleased to find local, customized recommendations and offers. With ease, Lily creates an online profile for her family, completes the forms, and selects her preferences including obtaining the family’s prior health data, entering her health goals, and accesses the mobile health app.

Finding a PCP Fast

Lily thinks she has pinkeye and searches Simply Health for a provider, requesting a same-day appointment. She is offered several diagnostic and pricing options from which to selects. Every option will allow her provider access to her medical records.

Selecting a Same-Day Option

Using her promotion code, she selects a home visit, and enters the requested information. After entering her code, she pays the remaining amount using her HSA and receives payment confirmation. Lily receives information and a picture of her caregiver.

Conducting the Appointment

Within 20 minutes the caregiver completes the examination and sends a prescription to Lily’s preferred pharmacy. The caregiver suggests Lily connect her FITBIT to her Simply Health app which allows Lily to earn loyalty points, and show her how to make the connection.

Post-Visit Interaction

After the visit, Lily learns she can view her payment online and rate the visit. She happily posts about her experience. Her prescription arrives along with daily reminders to take it.

Selecting a PCP, Making an Appointment

Lily is notified to select her PCP and to schedule a wellness visit. Logging into Simply Health, she reviews the recommended providers, selects a PCP, and chooses an appointment time. She receives a confirmation message which asks her to confirm her data, and allows her to download a check-in pass. Lily requests to receive an automated visit reminder.

Real-Time Convenience

Lily is alerted that her PCP is running late. As a result she is offered four free options regarding her appointment. Online, Lily selects an option, and receives Simply Health loyalty points. She is so impressed with the ease of the process she shares it with her social network.
